What We Found

During our review of your IT Glue environment, we identified several areas that need attention. These are common challenges we see in MSPs that have set up IT Glue but never fully implemented it.

Integrations Pulling Incorrectly

Tools that depend on IT Glue are not getting the right data. Lifecycle cannot identify hardware refresh and warranty opportunities because configurations are not structured correctly. Other integrations have the same problem: bad data in, bad data out.

Assets & Configurations

Asset types are not structured for how your business operates. The question "are we using assets the right way?" applies across the board: configurations, flexible assets, and how data is categorized.

Site Summaries & Topologies

Site summaries and network topologies are not set up to give your team the quick overview they need per client. These are the first things a technician should see when opening a customer record.

Documentation Spread Across Systems

Critical information lives in IT Glue, SharePoint, Teams, and other places. Without a clear decision on what goes where, your team wastes time searching and still does not trust what they find.

SOPs & Checklists

IT Glue has built-in SOPs and checklists, but they are not being used. Standard procedures live in people's heads instead of being documented and linked to the right assets and organizations.

AI & Automation Readiness

The end goal is running AI and automations on top of IT Glue data: employee onboarding, role changes, standard procedures. That requires clean, structured, and consistently formatted data as the foundation. The current state does not support it.

Questions & Answers

How long does the reimplementation take?

This depends on the size of your client base and your team's capacity. Most reimplementations take between 3 and 6 months. We determine the pace together during kick-off.

Do you configure everything for us?

We design the architecture, build the templates, and configure the integrations. Your team handles the client-specific data entry. We guide and advise throughout, but you own the knowledge.

What if we already have data in IT Glue?

We work with what is there. Clean data gets migrated into the new structure. Outdated data gets flagged for review. We do not delete anything without your approval.

What happens after the implementation project?

After go-live, you can optionally continue with Guide as a Service: monthly guidance covering optimization, automation, training, and ongoing support. The initial GaaS term is 3 months, then it renews monthly until written cancellation with a one-month notice period. There is no obligation to sign up for GaaS when accepting this proposal.

Can we add Power BI dashboards later?

Yes. Once the data is clean and structured, we can connect Power BI for operational dashboards. This is a separate add-on at €300/month, typically added after the foundation is in place.
