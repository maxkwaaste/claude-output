Five specific mistakes

Mistake 1: Generic Keywords John added broad terms like "msp billing software", "msp patch management software", "msp rmm software", "best psa software for msp". These are high-volume, high-competition terms where Proxuma isn't the answer. Proxuma solves Autotask project planning pain, not generic MSP tooling needs.
Mistake 2: Maximize Clicks Instead of Conversions Your original campaign used "Maximize Conversions" which optimizes for people who actually sign up. John's campaigns used "Maximize Clicks" which just drives traffic. At $6.39/click with zero conversions, that's Google gladly taking your money for junk clicks.
Mistake 3: Budget Explosion From $10/day on one focused campaign to $66/day on three broad campaigns ($198/day total potential). 20x the daily budget for zero return.
Mistake 4: Constant Status Toggling 228 status changes in 3 months. Google's algorithm needs 2-4 weeks of stable data to optimize. Constantly pausing and unpausing campaigns resets the learning phase every time.
Mistake 5: No Negative Keywords Your entire strategy was built on negative keyword discipline: blocking irrelevant searches. John added zero negative keywords to his new campaigns. Every "msp software" search from someone looking for RMM or backup tools hit your ads.
