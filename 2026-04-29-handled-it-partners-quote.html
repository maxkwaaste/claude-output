Our Approach

Step 1: Intake session Done

We started with a discovery session on your current way of working and how Autotask is set up. Based on that intake we have a good understanding of your current situation: the migration from ConnectWise, the gaps in SLA configuration, ticket categorization, and integration challenges. Using our experience across 150+ MSP implementations, we can already point to several quick wins.

Step 2: Kick-off

Once the quote is approved, we will host a kick-off with the project team. Within the kick-off we brief the project team. We go into the goals, project plan, first actions and will finalize the way we're going to work together.

Step 3: Execution

Based on the intake, we will follow our structured method for optimizing Autotask and related topics. We start with the service offering (menu) and the processes, then move through integrations, enforcement, and KPI dashboards. We do this in sprints of 2 weeks. For each sprint we define the correct actions and who is responsible. Every sprint we review what was done, course-correcting along the way.

Step 4: Go-live

We decide on the go-live date together. Before the go-live date we double check everything to make sure it is working as intended. Dxfferent can provide a custom training for your employees in one or two sessions, getting everyone on board with the new way of working.

Step 5: Optimize

If you are still happy with our services we can continue and touch all parts of your MSP. The areas we could work on include: data-driven decision making, Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC), Identity, Professionalism, Portfolio, Automation, Positioning, Scalability and Technology.

Our Services

Guide as a Service (GaaS)

With GaaS, you always have an MSP guide by your side. This MSP consultant is available once every two weeks to help your business, and serves as a sparring partner for all MSP-related issues. This could be in the area of Autotask, ISO, contracts, services or other strategic issues. Our consultants come from the MSP field and have years of experience in various positions. They have helped dozens of other MSPs transform their business through Dxfferent.

In addition to the biweekly consultations, your consultant is available on an unlimited basis via Teams for fair-use questions about related topics. The consultant interacts with either one or a few main contacts, but will not act as a point of contact for your entire organization.

Buddy as a Service (BaaS) — Premium Option

Want to move faster? Buddy as a Service is our hands-on acceleration package. Everything in GaaS, plus your consultant meets with you weekly instead of biweekly, takes on more of the configuration and implementation work directly, and provides priority response on Teams. This is for MSPs who want the fastest possible path to results without having to do all the homework themselves.
