What are riblappen? In English butchery: chuck steak, braising steak, or stewing steak. Cut from the shoulder/chuck area. Tough, fatty, full of connective tissue. Cheap because most buyers want tender cuts. Nutritionally dense because of the collagen and fat content. Perfect for slow cooking or pan-searing as a daily steak.

Recommendation

Best overall: Farm2Fork Braising Steak -- certified wild-grazed (AGW + Pasture for Life), frozen, individually packed steaks, free delivery over 100. You already use them. Order 200 worth and you get ~40 days of daily steaks ready to pull from the freezer.


Best value if you can portion yourself: Fordhall Farm Bulk Buy -- 10% cheaper at 17.99/kg, wholly grass-fed, but comes in 5kg blocks. Ask them about individual portioning before ordering.


Maximum volume play: Sherwood Foods -- 3x the meat for the same money, frozen and vacuum sealed. But it is Irish beef and grain-finishing status is unclear. Email wholesale@sherwoodfoods.co.uk to confirm before ordering.

Suppliers that did NOT make the cut Eversfield Organic: No braising/chuck steak listed. Turner & George: Premium London butcher, braising steak not stocked. Field & Flower: Rotating "cut of the week" model, can't reliably order chuck. Farmison & Co: Website blocks automated access (check manually). The Grassfed Collective / Native Breeds: Don't exist as standalone retailers.
