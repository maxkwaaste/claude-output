3. Structural Issues

These need your decision before ad changes go live.

New campaigns only target UK

The 3 new campaigns (Brand, Autotask Features, Recorded Demo) only serve ads in the UK. Your legacy campaigns cover NL. Nobody covers US or Australia. The May 5 audit flagged this. Should I add US (2840) + AU (2036) + NL (2528) geo targets to the new campaigns?

Legacy vs new campaigns: which to keep?

Legacy "Targeted campaign autotask" gets all the traffic (249 impr) but has 900+ keywords, many garbage. New "Autotask Features" has clean keyword lists but barely gets impressions. Two options: (A) Fix the new campaigns (geo + ad copy) and pause legacy, or (B) keep both running. Running both means campaigns compete against each other in the auction.
