1
Fix Conversion Double-Counting
10 min Blocking

Why first: Right now, every demo booking is counted 2-3 times in Google Ads. This inflates your conversion numbers and will confuse automated bidding the moment you turn it on. Nothing else matters until tracking is clean.

What's wrong: Three enabled conversion actions fire for the same booking event:

  • "Booked a demo (proxuma.io/)" -- codeless page view (ID 7239093290)
  • "Page view (bookingconfirm)" -- codeless page view (ID 7219443053)
  • "bookingconfirmed" -- GA4 event (ID 7219607704)

Same duplication for recorded demo requests (page view + GA4 event).

What to do: In Google Ads UI > Goals > Conversions > Summary:

  1. Keep "Booked a demo (proxuma.io/)" as Primary, counting = One
  2. Keep "Page view (recorded-demo-requested)" as Primary, counting = One
  3. Set "bookingconfirm page view" to Secondary (or remove)
  4. Set "bookingconfirmed" GA4 to Secondary
  5. Set "recordeddemorequested" GA4 to Secondary
  6. Set "Google Ads | Mail Click" to Secondary (low-value action)

Result: exactly 2 primary conversion actions. Clean signal for bidding.

Switch to Automated Bidding (After 15+ Conversions)
Week 4-6 Scale Only after data

Why last: Automated bidding needs conversion data to work. Without 15-30 conversions, Google's algorithm guesses badly and overspends. This is the step that killed John's campaigns -- he used Maximize Clicks from day one on cold keywords.

When to switch:

  • You have 15+ conversions total across the account
  • You have a clear CPA benchmark (from the Manual CPC weeks)
  • Conversion tracking is clean (Step 1 verified)

How to switch:

  1. Change "Proxuma - Autotask Features" campaign from Manual CPC to Maximize Conversions
  2. Set a target CPA at 20% above your current average CPA (gives Google room to learn)
  3. Keep Brand campaign on Manual CPC permanently
  4. Keep Recorded Demo on Manual CPC until it has its own 15+ conversions
  5. Monitor for 2 weeks. If CPA spikes above 2x your target, revert to Manual CPC.
