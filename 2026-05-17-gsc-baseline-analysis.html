Trend insight Average position improved dramatically from ~40 in mid-2025 to ~9-12 in 2026. But CTR didn't follow: you're ranking better for more queries, yet click-through rates dropped. This means you're now showing up for broad queries where your titles and descriptions don't compel a click. Position improved, relevance signal didn't.

Optimization Priorities

Ranked by expected click impact, from highest to lowest leverage.

Priority 1: Rewrite the two comparison pages

Rewrite /the-best-msp-tools/ and /best-psa-software-for-msps-in-2023/
Potential: 500-1,000 additional clicks/month
These pages get 106K combined impressions at position 50-63. They need to be rewritten from scratch as proper, current comparison guides with updated dates, structured data, and proper internal linking. Moving from position 60 to position 10 on "msp tools" alone would mean 300+ new monthly clicks. Remove "2023" from the URL. Add table-of-contents, comparison tables, pros/cons sections. This is the single highest-impact action.
High effortHighest impact

Priority 2: Fix meta titles for zero-click top-position queries

Audit and rewrite meta titles/descriptions for 10 high-position queries
Potential: 50-100 additional clicks/month
Queries like "autotask capacity planning" (position 3, 1.2K impressions, 0 clicks) suggest the title shown in search doesn't match what the user wants. Check which page Google surfaces for these queries and rewrite the meta title to directly answer the implied question. "Autotask Capacity Planning: How to Set Up Resource Planning" beats a generic page title.
Low effortMedium impact

Priority 3: Create dedicated pages for high-impression gaps

Write new content for Autotask Kanban, Autotask News, What is Autotask PSA
Potential: 30-80 additional clicks/month
"Autotask kanban" gets 1,124 impressions at position 14 with no dedicated page. "What is Autotask PSA" gets 777 impressions at position 8 with no dedicated explainer. These are content gaps where a targeted article would likely rank page 1 quickly given existing domain authority for Autotask queries.
Medium effortMedium impact

Priority 4: Power BI report page titles

Rewrite meta titles for top 20 Power BI report pages
Potential: 20-50 additional clicks/month
Pages like "span-of-control" (1,362 impressions, position 4.5, 0 clicks) are visible in search but titled like internal reports. Change "Span of Control" to "Span of Control Dashboard for MSPs | How Many Direct Reports per Manager" to match search intent. Quick wins with minimal effort.
Low effortLow-medium impact

Priority 5: Internal linking strategy

Build internal links from high-authority pages to comparison pages
Potential: accelerates priority 1
The 8 how-to articles that get traffic should link to the comparison pages. The homepage (1,276 clicks) should link prominently to the best-tools content. Internal link equity is the fastest way to push those comparison pages from position 60 to position 20. Combined with content rewrites, this could push them to page 1.
