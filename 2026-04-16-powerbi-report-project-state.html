The report library

Eight hundred seventy-two Power BI insight reports are live at proxuma.io/powerbi/insights/, split 449 English and 423 Dutch. All are on the V5 "Printed Document" format — teal gradient frame, numbered sections, collapsible DAX per section, FAQ accordion — wrapped in the full marketing shell (morph bar, search hero, series nav, pipeline overview, related reports, CTA). Repo is Proxuma/powerbi-reports, last big commit 329d666 on April 6. Six pillar pages anchor the breadcrumbs: ticketing, costs, projects, client-reports, upsell, security. The editorial pipeline in autotask-content-planner.html now holds 281 planned reports, including 55 recent cross-source additions that combine two or three data sources.

The live dashboards

One hundred seventy-three self-contained interactive HTML dashboards sit at /powerbi/dashboards/ under WordPress parent 4503. Chart.js 4.4.7, DAX-sourced data embedded as JSON, no backend. A LaunchAgent on M1 Pro refreshes them daily at 07:00 via refresh.py. Coverage spans SLA, CSAT, ticket ops, financial, billable/hours, security, client health, projects, and executive views, with every EN insight article banner-linked to its dashboard and every NL article to "Interactief Dashboard".

The validation layer (Truthifier)

Two parallel trust tracks are running. DAX Truthifier (data correctness) hit 76 of 486 topics by April 13; Opus self-review on topics 56–76 passed clean — zero hallucinated tables, columns, or measures. Portfolio invariants have reconciled: €17.61M revenue, 118 resources, 67,521 tickets, 279 projects. Visual Truthifier inspected 105 of 872 reports on April 14 and caught a systemic GIANT_ARROW_CTA layout bug in the bottom viewport.

Open issues

Bottom line

Volume is shipped; trust is the next unlock.

The library and dashboards give Proxuma more public Power BI surface than any Dutch MSP competitor. The Truthifier work is what makes it defensible — every number traceable to DAX, every layout visually verified. Finishing that sweep is what separates a content farm from an analyst asset.
