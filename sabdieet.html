Guiding Principles

Philosophy This protocol treats pregnancy as a peak athletic event demanding the highest caliber of biological preparation. It combines the carnivore-ancestral framework (Saladino, Brecka) with the traditional-foods wisdom of Weston A. Price. Where they conflict, WAPF overrides -- except on grains, which remain eliminated. The result is a nutrient-dense, animal-based diet with traditional preparation methods for maximum bioavailability.
  • Both parents follow the same protocol for 6+ months before conception Both
  • Whole-food nutrients over isolated supplements wherever possible WAPF
  • Fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, K2) are the priority nutrients, delivered together through cod liver oil + butter WAPF
  • Organ meats are the most nutrient-dense foods on the planet Both
  • No industrial food: zero seed oils, zero refined sugar, zero synthetic additives Both
  • No grains: anti-nutrients (phytic acid, lectins) bind minerals and impair absorption OB Override

Supplement Stack

WAPF Override Cod liver oil replaces three separate supplements from the original Brecka stack: isolated fish oil (omega-3), vitamin D3, and vitamin A. The whole-food form delivers all three together with natural co-factors. The remaining supplements below fill gaps that food alone may not cover.
#SupplementFormWhy
1 High-vitamin cod liver oil 2 tsp/day 20,000 IU retinol + 2,000 IU D3 + 2g omega-3 (EPA/DHA). The cornerstone. Take with butter to balance EPA with ARA.
2 Methylfolate (5-MTHF) L-5-MTHF, not folic acid Bypasses MTHFR mutations (44-60% of population affected). Fetal DNA synthesis, neural tube closure. Remove ALL synthetic folic acid from diet and supplements.
3 Methylcobalamin (B12) Methylated form Cellular energy, red blood cells, fetal neurological function. Food sources (liver, fish eggs) provide most needs; supplement as insurance.
4 P-5-P (Active B6) Pyridoxal-5-phosphate Homocysteine metabolism, pregnancy nausea management, neurotransmitter synthesis for maternal mood stability.
5 Magnesium glycinate Chelated form Calms CNS, promotes deep sleep, supports 300+ enzymatic functions. Evening dose. Also use magnesium baths.
6 Desiccated beef organs Capsules (grass-fed) Insurance for days when fresh liver is not consumed. Provides retinol, choline, heme iron, B-vitamins in bioavailable form.
Do not take Standard prenatal vitamins -- contain synthetic folic acid (blocks folate receptors in MTHFR carriers) and synthetic vitamin A (linked to birth defects). Also contain poorly absorbed mineral forms. Replace entirely with the stack above + the food protocol.
EPA/ARA Balance Warning Cod liver oil contains EPA which can cause hemorrhaging during birth if not balanced by arachidonic acid (ARA). ARA is found in liver, egg yolks, and meat fats. This is why the daily protocol includes 4 tbsp butter, 2+ eggs, and meat with fat -- they provide the ARA counterbalance. Do not take cod liver oil on a low-fat or plant-based diet.

Trimester-Specific Priorities

1st Trimester -- Now through 8 July 2026
Foundation: Neural Tube & Organ Formation
  • Priority nutrients: Choline, methylfolate, B12, retinol (vitamin A)
  • For nausea: Raw dairy (kefir, hard cheese), egg yolks, desiccated organ capsules, bone broth
  • Vitamin A is critical now -- necessary for differentiation and patterning of all cells, tissues, and organs
  • Continue cod liver oil through nausea. If oral is difficult, try frozen capsules.
2nd Trimester -- 9 July through 21 October 2026
Growth: Blood Volume & Skeletal Development
  • Increase: Raw dairy (calcium, K2), red meat (heme iron for expanding blood volume), saturated fats for hormones
  • Monitor glucose with CGM as physiological insulin resistance rises (natural adaptation to shuttle glucose to fetus)
  • Post-meal walks: 10 min after eating to blunt glucose spikes
  • Iron caution: Ferritin drops as blood volume expands -- don't panic if hemoglobin stays optimal. Replete through dietary heme iron (red meat, liver) first, not synthetic ferrous sulfate
3rd Trimester -- 22 October 2026 through 21 January 2027
Finishing: Brain Architecture & Birth Prep
  • Heavy marine omega-3s (EPA/DHA) for final brain and retinal development -- cod liver oil + SMASH fish
  • Blood sugar stability: Frequent smaller animal-based meals
  • High protein for tissue integrity before birth
  • Glycine-rich foods: Bone broth, skin, slow-cooked meats for connective tissue preparation
  • Vitamin K2 from aged cheese + butter for blood clotting readiness

Complete Elimination List

Food & Drink

  1. Folic acid -- check ALL labels, discard anything "enriched" or "fortified"
  2. Industrial seed oils -- canola, soybean, sunflower, grapeseed, safflower, "vegetable oil"
  3. Refined sugar & white carbs -- white flour, white rice, processed sugars
  4. All grains -- including "properly prepared" soaked/sprouted (anti-nutrients still present)
  5. Legumes -- beans, lentils, peanuts (lectins, phytic acid)
  6. Nuts & seeds -- poorly digestible, high in phytic acid and omega-6
  7. Soy -- phytoestrogens, anti-nutrients, almost always GMO
  8. Tap water -- fluoride is a neurotoxin that crosses the placenta
  9. Large predatory fish -- tuna, swordfish, shark (mercury bioaccumulation)
  10. Farm-raised salmon -- toxic
  11. Standard prenatal vitamins -- synthetic forms

Environment

  1. Phthalates & parabens -- conventional lotions, shampoos, cosmetics. Switch to beef tallow-based skincare, organic essential oils.
  2. Synthetic fragrances -- "fragrance"/"parfum" on labels hides hundreds of untested chemicals. No chemical candles, air fresheners, fabric softener.
  3. Plastics (BPA/BPS) -- no heating food in plastic, no soft plastic bottles. Glass, stainless steel, cast iron only.
  4. Non-stick cookware -- PFAS. Use cast iron, stainless steel, enameled cast iron.

Water Hierarchy

  1. Hydrogen water (molecular H2 as selective antioxidant)
  2. Spring water (naturally mineral-rich)
  3. Filtered/RO water remineralized with Celtic sea salt

Postpartum Recovery (First 30 Days)

After placenta delivery: massive rapid drop in estrogen and progesterone. The hormonal axis takes approximately 30 days to begin recovering. During this window:

  • Continue cod liver oil -- high-dose marine omega-3s prevent postpartum depression and support endocrine reboot
  • Continue methylfolate, D3, magnesium -- maintain the full supplement stack
  • Organ meats daily -- heme iron for blood loss replacement, choline and B-vitamins for lactation and cellular energy
  • Collagen and bone broth -- glycine and proline for pelvic floor repair, connective tissue, uterine wall healing
  • Raw dairy and animal fats -- high-fat raw kefir, raw butter, beef tallow for rebuilding hormones and enriching breast milk lipid profile
  • Gentle movement only -- breathwork, gentle walking, basic pelvic floor connection. No direct core work. Breath-led Pilates flows after 2-3 weeks.

The Protocol in One Paragraph

Eat nose-to-tail from pasture-raised animals: liver daily, eggs daily, raw dairy daily, red meat with the fat daily, SMASH fish and shellfish multiple times per week, bone broth as your cooking liquid. Take 2 tsp high-vitamin cod liver oil with 4 tbsp grass-fed butter. Add lacto-fermented condiments and fish roe. Supplement only methylfolate, methylated B-vitamins, and magnesium glycinate. Eliminate all grains, legumes, nuts, seeds, seed oils, refined sugar, synthetic vitamins, tap water, and environmental toxins. Move daily: strength 2x/week, Pilates 3-4x/week, walk after every meal. Sleep 8-9 hours in a dark room. Get morning sunlight. Both parents follow this for 6+ months before conception and throughout pregnancy.
