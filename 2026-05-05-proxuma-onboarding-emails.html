HubSpot Template Usage

How to import

1. In HubSpot, go to Marketing > Email > Create email > Regular

2. Choose "Code your own" tab

3. Paste the HTML source from below

4. Replace {{contact.firstname}} and {{company.name}} with HubSpot personalization tokens (they'll auto-populate)

5. Update the REGISTRATION_URL placeholder with your actual Proxuma registration link or HubSpot CTA

Placeholders to fill
  • REGISTRATION_URL - Your Proxuma registration/signup link
  • MASTER_GUIDE_URL - Link to the onboarding master guide document
  • LOGO_URL - Hosted Proxuma logo (use: https://proxuma.io/wp-content/uploads/proxuma-logo.png or your CDN)
  • {{contact.firstname}} - HubSpot auto-fills the contact's first name
  • {{company.name}} - HubSpot auto-fills the company name
