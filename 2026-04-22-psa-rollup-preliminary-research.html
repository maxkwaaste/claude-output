Quick finding: the big roll-ups are probably under-represented by brand search

Why this matters Direct keyword match of Evergreen/Lyra, New Charter, and The 20 MSP's known portfolio brands against the 413 list yielded only 2 hits (Van Roey ICT Group flagged as possible Evergreen match by coincidence, and Impact Group flagged by domain — neither confirmed). That's because the published brand lists on our side are incomplete — Evergreen has 100+ acquisitions but only a dozen named publicly, and they explicitly keep acquired brands intact. The only way to catch them is per-account web search — visit each company's About or Legal page, or check Crunchbase / PitchBook for ownership.
