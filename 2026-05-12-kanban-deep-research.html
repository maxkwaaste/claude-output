Architecture Overview

The Kanban system is entity-agnostic. A KanBanAdapter interface abstracts data fetching, card rendering, and move operations. The board components (VKanBan*) are shared across all modules. Each domain plugs in its own adapter.

Key architecture decision No external drag-and-drop library. Native HTML5 draggable="true" with custom composables (useKanbanDnd, useKanbanCardDrag, useAutoScroll). Keeps the bundle small and avoids library coupling.

The 5 Adapters

1. Deal Pipeline dealAdapter.ts
CRM deal pipeline with HubSpot sync. 13 column options (Leverancier, Lead/Misschien, Voorstel/Plannen, Wacht op klant, 5 Active variants, SO Abonnement, Marketing, Completed, Closed lost). Card fields include amount (currency), company name (clickable link), close date, activity indicator (green/yellow/red by recency), deal source, trial status, enrichment data. Two presets: "Pipeline Manager" and "Minimal". Swimlanes: company_name (cross-swimlane blocked), deal_source.
HubSpot Sync 13 Columns 2 Presets
2. Jira Issues jiraAdapter.ts
Development tracking with 24 Jira statuses mapped to columns (Open through QA workflows to Gereed/Afgewezen). Card fields: story points, assignee, epic summary (badge), issue type (badge), priority, sprint, last update. Swimlanes: sprint, epic, assignee, issue type. Read-only: moves are no-ops. No deep links (cards open IssueDetailSlideover per decision D4).
24 Statuses Read-only 4 Swimlane Options
3. Internal Roadmap roadmapAdapter.ts
Product roadmap with 11 statuses (backlog through on_prod). Card fields: status label, brand, MRR (calculated from pricing), epics count, target date, category. Swimlanes: brand, category. Read-only, no deep links.
11 Statuses Read-only
4. Client Actions clientActionAdapter.ts
Assessment actions from MyDxfferent. 10 statuses (proposed through rejected/postponed/skipped). Writable: moves update action status via PUT. Card fields: priority (colored badge), maturity level range (L1-L5), pillar, subject, owner, deadline, evidence count, phase. 5 swimlane options, 3 presets (Standaard, Uitgebreid, Compact). Cross-swimlane allowed except for pillar grouping.
Writable 10 Statuses 3 Presets
5. Client Roadmap clientRoadmapAdapter.ts
Client-facing roadmap items. 4 statuses (backlog, planned, in_progress, completed). Card fields: pillar name, action count, target quarter, priority (MoSCoW), health indicator (on_track/risk/off_track), linked actions (list with badges). Callback-based moves. Swimlanes: pillar only.
Callback Moves 4 Statuses

Design System Integration

Theme Support

The Kanban components use NuxtUI semantic tokens, which means they automatically adapt to all 6 themes: Proxuma (light), Proxuma Dark, Dxfferent (dark), Dxfferent Light, NeXum (dark), NeXum Light. No component-level theme overrides needed.
