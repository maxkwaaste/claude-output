What's Broken (in Andy's words)

No KPIs, No SLAs
SLAs not set properly. No KPIs defined or measured. Andy built a basic morning huddle dashboard last week but needs help with the KPIs he can't figure out how to measure in Autotask. Starting to drive daily huddles with the team next Monday.
No Dashboards (useful ones)
No useful dashboards. Built one for the morning huddle but needs role-based dashboards with proper data enforcement so numbers are accurate. Engineers aren't logging time on tickets consistently.
No Client Reports
Zero reports going out to clients. No QBR process in place.
No Process Charts / Flows
No ticket triage. Everything comes in as a generic "service ticket" -- no categorization, no differentiated workflow. Working on defining expectations around ticket creation (required fields, when to escalate, what statuses mean) but no flow charts exist.
No Automations
"Almost no automations built out to help the team." They're operating at a very basic level. Need workflow rules to enforce time entry, ticket hygiene, and process compliance.
Noisy RMM Alerts
Monitors board full of noise. Lead supervisor is only one who checks it and says "there's a lot of noise." Andy wants alerts pruned so only actionable items remain, then routed to the main service board where all eyes can see them. We offered templates for this.
Billing Chaos
QuickBooks not integrated. Manual invoice adjustments every few months. Clients not standardized on offerings. Andy: "that's got to end." Max promised "no more incorrect invoices" once integrated.
Missing Integrations
Only Datto RMM integrated with Autotask. No 365 integration for clients, no QuickBooks, no HubSpot, no ScalePad. "Our hands are tied."
No AI Adoption by Team
Andy uses AI daily, but the team doesn't. A senior technician called himself a "Google genius" -- hasn't tried Gemini despite paying for it. Cooper AI in Autotask not being used. Copilot not being used.
