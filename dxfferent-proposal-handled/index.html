Our Approach

01
Intake Done

We started with a discovery session on your current way of working and how Autotask is set up. Based on that intake we have a good understanding of your current situation.

02
Kick-off

Once the quote is approved, we will host a kick-off with the project team. We go into the goals, project plan, first actions and will finalize the way we work together.

03
Execution

We work in sprints of 2 weeks. For each sprint we define the correct actions and who is responsible. Every sprint we review and course-correct.

04
Go-live

We decide on the go-live date together. Before go-live we double check everything. Dxfferent can provide custom training for your employees in one or two sessions.

05
Optimize

If you are still happy with our services we can continue and touch all parts of your MSP: data-driven decision making, GRC, portfolio, automation, positioning, scalability.

Programme

We work in a phased approach. The exact scope and timeline will be determined together during kick-off with our lead consultant. How fast we move depends on your team's capacity and how large you want the scope to be.

Phase Focus What We Cover
Phase 1 Foundation The foundation for your billing, processes, and how that translates into Autotask. This covers your ticket processes and how they are configured, including SLA and contract setup. We start with the service offering (menu) and the critical processes, then work through all related topics within our programme.
Phase 2 Dashboards & Training What we built in Phase 1, we translate into dashboards for your team. Role-specific views for technicians, service managers, and leadership. We help with training to get everyone on board with the new way of working.
Phase 3 Continuous Improvement Based on what we built in the previous phases, we continue to optimize and expand. This could include project management, a BI module, additional integrations, advanced automations, or any other area where your MSP can grow.

Note: This is a collaboration. We guide, you implement. The pace depends on your team and the scope you want to cover. We determine this together during kick-off. Our goal is a lasting transformation, not a checkbox exercise.
