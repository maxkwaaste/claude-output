What we connect

Data we pull from Autotask

Every entity below is synced into Power BI and available for dashboards, reports, and AI analysis. We never write back to Autotask — our API access is strictly read-only.

Tickets

All ticket types, statuses, priorities, queues, SLA targets, and resolution data. Full lifecycle from creation to close.

Contracts

Recurring contracts, block hours, T&M agreements, contract periods, billing codes, and renewal dates.

Projects

Project phases, tasks, milestones, estimated vs. actual hours, budget tracking, and project status.

Time Entries

All billable and non-billable time, per resource, per ticket or project. The foundation of profitability analysis.

Billing & Revenue

Invoice items, revenue by client, cost rates, billing rates, and the gap between estimated and actual revenue.

Companies

Client master data, classification, territory, account managers, and custom UDFs you've configured in Autotask.

Resources

Team members, roles, departments, cost rates, and capacity. Essential for utilisation and KPI reporting.

Configuration Items

Devices, hardware, software assets, warranty dates, and lifecycle status. Cross-reference with RMM for full asset visibility.

SLAs & Queues

SLA definitions, response targets, resolution targets, queue assignments, and escalation rules.
