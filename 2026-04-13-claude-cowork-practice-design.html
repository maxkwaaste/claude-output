Claude Cowork Practice for Dutch SMBs

Bottom line

The Dutch Claude specialist

Not AI transformation. Not tool-agnostic. Claude only, because it's the best tool for knowledge work and nobody in the Dutch SMB market is speaking that language yet. Credibility comes from running Proxuma and Dxfferent on Claude every day. The audit is the wedge, the rollout is the payday, the retainer is the compounding revenue.

1. Brand and positioning

Positioning. Claude-only specialist. Dutch-first marketing and delivery, English available for internationally-oriented SMBs. Credibility angle: Max runs his SaaS and consulting practice on Claude. Every workshop and LinkedIn post draws from real production work, not theory.

  • Helder AI (NL for "clear") — evokes Claude's voice, softer play
  • Max de Kwaasteniet — Claude voor Bedrijven — personal brand route

3. Signature methodology (the IP)

The thing that makes this a repeatable practice rather than freelancing. Same template every time, different content per customer.

The Readiness Scan framework
Five-dimension scoring model run during the audit. Use-case density, data hygiene, workflow ownership, change readiness, tech environment. Scored 1–5, produces a radar chart, drives the rollout roadmap. Branded deliverable format, Proxuma-style report.
The Champions program
Two to four internal power users trained intensively during rollout. They carry adoption after Max leaves. Proven playbook from Dxfferent's MSP onboarding work.
The Prompt Library template
Role-indexed markdown library, same structure every engagement (sales, ops, finance, admin, leadership), customized during rollout. Shipped as a private GitHub repo or Notion workspace per customer.
The Weekly Use-Case Drop
Every Monday, a short Dutch briefing of one new Claude use case relevant to SMBs. Drives retainer stickiness and doubles as LinkedIn content. One asset, two purposes.
