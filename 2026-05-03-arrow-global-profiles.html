The SA Jewish Pipeline to London Property Finance

Research by the Kaplan Centre at UCT documents a South African Jewish community of 10,000-15,000 in London, concentrated in NW London (Camden, Barnet, Harrow). A Jewish Chronicle article describes a "South African mafia" in London business. The pipeline runs: Herzlia (Cape Town) or King David (Johannesburg) to Investec (the SA Jewish bank) to London specialist finance. Sher's career follows this pipeline precisely. The community is tight enough that school connections persist across decades and continents.

Corrections from Deep Research

  • Jonathan Samuels is British, not South African. Born Feb 1978. Oxford PPE, McKinsey, Standard Chartered. His connection to Posniak is a London professional relationship (Dragonfly 2009+), not an SA community one.
  • Posniak family likely from Cape Town. SA Yellow Pages lists "Posniak" at Sea Point, Cape Town -- the historic Jewish neighborhood. This supports the hypothesis that Posniak and Sher grew up in the same Cape Town Jewish community.
