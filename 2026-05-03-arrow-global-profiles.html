Head of Lending, Arrow Global (ex-CEO Maslow Capital)
Born ~1974
South African / British

Background

Origin
South Africa, almost certainly Cape Town area. First job after university was articled clerk at Grant Thornton in Cape Town, the standard post-study placement.
Education
University in South Africa (likely UCT, University of Cape Town). Qualified as Chartered Accountant through the South African route: 4-year degree plus 3-year articles.
Ethnicity
South African Jewish (Litvak). "Sher" is a well-documented Lithuanian Jewish surname in the South African community. The Pretoria Sher family on JewishGen traces back to Shavl (Siauliai), Lithuania and Riga, Latvia.
Religion
Not publicly stated. The South African Jewish community is predominantly Orthodox in affiliation but often secular in practice.
Family
Married (wife referenced in 2007 Mortgage Strategy profile). Lives in Hampstead, London (as of 2007).
Interests
Cricket: plays for the "Green Mambas," a London team of South African expats. Golf. Counting Crows fan. Single malt whisky and Cabernet Sauvignon.

Network: Where They First Connected

Ellis Sher is the central node. Every other person in this group connects to the network through him. The Cheval Property Finance era (2005-2008) is where the core relationships formed.

Network analysis Sher is the hub. He connects to every other person in the group through direct working relationships. The Cheval era (2005-2008) is the key formation period: Sher and Halpern came from Investec together, Posniak joined them at Cheval. Sher then founded Maslow (2009), where Steele joined as COO (2017). When Arrow acquired Maslow in 2023, Sher brought both Posniak and the Maslow team into Lewy's orbit. Lewy and Sher had no confirmed prior connection before the acquisition.
