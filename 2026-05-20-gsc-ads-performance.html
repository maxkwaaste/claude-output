Bottom line

Spend is up, conversions are flat.

€165.95 across 30 days for 1 conversion. The brand campaign is the only one earning its keep. The targeted autotask campaign is burning cash at €15.20 per click with nothing to show. Two campaigns sit dormant. GSC has 22K impressions queued up just outside page one, waiting on a positional nudge to convert into clicks.

Next moves.

  1. Watch the targeted autotask CPC. If it stays above €10 with zero conversions through next week, pause or rebuild it.
  2. Confirm the May 19 spike is real. Pull data again in 3 days. If activity holds, the South Africa segment may be a useful geo to lean into.
  3. Diagnose the dead campaigns. Check Proxuma — Brand and Proxuma — Recorded Demo for budget, bid, or eligibility issues.
  4. Pick 5 GSC keywords ranking 8 to 15. Push position improvements on those pages. Page 2 to page 1 is where CTR gains hide.
