Notable Findings

Constellation / Vela footprint Vela Software Group (Constellation Software division) controls 3 accounts totalling 500 seats. Constellation's broader empire includes FOG Software Group and other verticals — these are long-hold, never-sell acquisitions. High churn risk for any competing vendor.
Opin Kerfi / OK chain (Iceland) Opin Kerfi hf. (now trading as OK) is PE-backed by VEX ehf., an Icelandic PE fund. OK itself acquired Premis ehf. in 2022 and TRS's IT division. Two Autotask accounts (Premis + Opin Kerfi) now sit under the same PE firm.
Recent acquisitions (2024-2026) Several accounts changed hands very recently: Datasharp → Focus Group (March 2024), JDM → itm8/Axcel PE (Denmark), Impact Group → Aavalar/Spruce Infotech (March 2026). These are live roll-up targets where incumbents may be re-evaluating vendor relationships.
Public-market parents Issys ICT (NL) → Dustin Group AB (Nasdaq Stockholm, Axel Johnson family). AddOns Inc (US) → COSOL Limited (ASX). Proact IT Group is itself Nasdaq-listed. These accounts are subject to procurement policies and budget cycles of publicly traded companies.

Methodology

Seven ordered probes per account: (1) homepage and about pages, (2) company/legal/imprint/colofon pages, (3) UK Companies House PSC register for UK accounts, (4) LinkedIn company sidebar via DDG SERP, (5) Crunchbase via DDG SERP, (6) acquisition/news press search, (7) PitchBook / OpenCorporates snippet search. For European accounts: French societe.com, Dutch KVK, Irish SoloCheck, Croatian fininfo.hr, Swedish allabolag.se, and Swiss Moneyhouse were used directly.

Classification by Claude Sonnet 4.6 with structured JSON verdict. Confidence rubric: high = own site or registry filing confirms; medium = one credible third party; low = weak or ambiguous signal. Research conducted April 22, 2026.
