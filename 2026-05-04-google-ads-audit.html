Why Conversions Stopped

Three things happened roughly at the same time:

1. NEW1 was paused (the best converter)
PAUSED AFTER JULY 2025
NEW1 was producing conversions at EUR 84/each. It was paused, and the account lost its most efficient campaign. The Autotask campaign alone couldn't sustain the conversion rate because it was spending most of its budget on non-converting keywords (Planning, Outlook, Email groups).
2. Autotask campaign CPC climbed from EUR 6 to EUR 17
PRICE DOUBLED IN 4 MONTHS
Average CPC went from EUR 6 (Jun 2025) to EUR 13 (Oct), EUR 13.4 (Dec), EUR 15 (Feb), EUR 17 (Jan). The market got more competitive for Autotask terms, or the Quality Score degraded. The "Planning" ad group alone spent EUR 235 in Oct-Nov on "Autotask planning" and "Autotask scheduling" with zero conversions.
3. John's takeover destroyed algorithm learning
614 CHANGES IN 3 MONTHS
John (b2bleads.nl) made 614 changes starting Feb 2026. Added generic MSP keywords, switched to Maximize Clicks bidding, launched 3 new campaigns at EUR 198/day budget. 228 status toggling changes reset the algorithm's learning. Zero conversions from any of John's campaigns.

Top 5 Actions (Ranked by Impact)

  1. Set up conversion tracking. It may have broken or never been properly set up. During the golden period there WERE conversions recorded, so something was tracking them. Check if the conversion action still exists in Google Ads and if the tag is still on proxuma.io. If it's gone, recreate it. Use the setup prompt provided separately.
  2. Rebuild the golden formula as a new campaign. Create a new campaign with ONLY the three keywords that converted: "Autotask Project Management" [phrase], "Autotask Project" [phrase], "Autotask Planning" [phrase]. Maximize Conversions bidding. EUR 10/day budget. One ad group. This is what NEW1 was, and it cost EUR 84/conversion.
  3. Pause or gut the Autotask campaign. Right now it's spending on Planning (0 conversions, EUR 235 in Oct-Nov), Outlook (0 conversions), and diluting the Project Management group's budget. Either pause the Planning and Outlook ad groups entirely, or pause the whole campaign and let the rebuilt campaign from action #2 replace it.
  4. Add negative keywords at account level. The full list above. This protects all campaigns, including any new ones you create.
  5. Mobile bid -50% on non-brand campaigns. Desktop is where B2B MSP buyers convert. The one mobile click on Autotask cost EUR 11 with nothing to show for it.

The Playbook

The golden period proved that Google Ads can work for Proxuma. The formula was simple: phrase-match Autotask keywords, Maximize Conversions bidding, modest budget. "Autotask Project Management" as a phrase match keyword produced the best conversions in both campaigns that tried it.

What killed it was a combination of pausing the best campaign (NEW1), CPC inflation on the Autotask campaign, and then John's takeover making everything worse.

The path back is clear: verify conversion tracking still works, rebuild the NEW1 formula, cut the dead weight from the Autotask campaign, and protect everything with negatives. Expected result: 3-5 conversions/month at EUR 80-120 each, on a total budget of EUR 15-20/day across Brand + one focused Autotask Project Management campaign.
