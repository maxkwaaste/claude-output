## 01. Trimester-by-Trimester Nutrition

An animal-based foundation, free of industrial seed oils, with monitoring throughout.

### The Animal-Based Paradigm (Dr. Saladino)

The most nutrient-dense human diet centres on ruminant meat, organs, raw dairy, fruit, and honey.

- **Grass-Fed Meat** - Heme iron, complete protein, B12, zinc, creatine
- **Organ Meats** - 0.5 oz fresh liver per day, the original prenatal multivitamin
- **Raw Dairy** - 1 quart (1L) daily from pasture-fed cows. Calcium, K2, A, intact enzymes
- **Fruit & Honey** - Clean carbohydrate, polyphenols, ready energy
- **Pasture-Raised Eggs** *(WAPF)* - 2-4 whole eggs daily + extra yolks. Choline, biotin, ARA
- **Grass-Fed Butter** *(WAPF)* - 4 tbsp daily. K2, retinol, butyrate. Balances EPA with ARA
- **Shellfish & Roe** *(WAPF)* - Oysters, mussels 2-4x/week. Fish roe 2-3x/week. Sacred fertility foods
- **Lacto-Fermented Foods** *(WAPF)* - Sauerkraut, kimchi, kvass with every meal. Probiotics, K2
- **Aged Cheese** *(WAPF)* - Daily. Gouda, Emmental, Brie for vitamin K2. Goat cheese preferred
- **Bone Broth** *(WAPF)* - Daily. Glycine is conditionally essential in pregnancy

### ELIMINATE: Industrial Seed Oils

Canola, soybean, sunflower, grapeseed, safflower, corn, cottonseed. These oxidise during processing and embed inflammatory linoleic acid into cell membranes.

**Cook with:** tallow, ghee, butter, coconut oil, extra virgin olive oil (cold use).

### Limit Anti-Nutrients

Grains and beans contain oxalates, phytic acid, and lectins that bind minerals like zinc, iron, and calcium, blocking their absorption. Reduce or eliminate during pregnancy.

### CGM Monitoring (Dr. Means)

A continuous glucose monitor reveals which foods spike your glucose. Physiological insulin resistance increases naturally in the second and third trimester to shunt glucose to the baby, so monitoring matters more, not less.

Aim to keep post-meal glucose under 120 mg/dL. Walk for 10 minutes after every meal to blunt the curve.

## 02. Targeted Supplementation

Form matters more than dose. The body responds to bioavailable, methylated nutrients.

### CRITICAL: Folic Acid vs Methylfolate

Between 44 and 60 percent of people carry a MTHFR mutation that prevents conversion of synthetic folic acid into the active form. Worse, unconverted folic acid **blocks the folate receptor**, starving the developing baby of the active folate it needs.

| | Status |
|---|--------|
| **AVOID: Folic Acid** | Synthetic, often added to fortified flour and standard prenatals |
| **TAKE: Methylfolate (5-MTHF)** | Bioavailable form, no conversion required |

### Core Stack (Brecka + WAPF)

| Supplement | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Methylfolate | Active folate, supports neural tube |
| Methylcobalamin B12 | Active B12 for methylation |
| P-5-P (B6) | Active B6 for nervous system |
| Magnesium Glycinate | Sleep, muscle, anti-cramp |
| High-Vitamin Cod Liver Oil *(WAPF)* | 2 tsp/day. 20K IU retinol + 2K IU D3 + 2g omega-3. Replaces separate D3, vit A, fish oil |
| Desiccated Beef Organs | Capsules for days without fresh liver. Retinol, choline, heme iron backup |

### Iron Caution

Avoid blanket synthetic iron prescriptions like ferrous sulfate. Get heme iron from red meat and liver first. Supplement only if labs confirm deficiency, and even then use a gentler form like iron bisglycinate.

### EPA/ARA Balance Warning *(WAPF)*

Cod liver oil contains EPA which can cause hemorrhaging during birth if not balanced by arachidonic acid (ARA). ARA comes from liver, egg yolks, and meat fats. This is why the protocol includes 4 tbsp butter, 2+ eggs, and meat with fat daily. Do not take cod liver oil on a low-fat or plant-based diet.

### Standard Prenatals *(WAPF)*

Avoid standard prenatal vitamins. They contain synthetic folic acid (blocks folate receptors in MTHFR carriers) and synthetic vitamin A (linked to birth defects). The cod liver oil + food protocol above replaces them entirely.

## 03. Movement & Strength Training

Pregnancy is not an illness. The strong body delivers, recovers, and carries the infant better.

### No Bed Rest (Dr. Ghulmiyyah)

Movement is medicine. The default prescription should be activity, not rest. **5 days per week, 30 min.**

### High-Intensity Strength (Mangan)

Slow sets to failure preserve and build muscle. Posterior chain focus protects the lower back. **2x per week, 30 min sessions.**

### Pregnancy Pilates (Bryony Deery)

Breath-led core work. No crunches or sit-ups. Elevated planks, side-lying series, pelvic floor coordination. **3-4x per week.**

### Weekly Framework

| Modality | Frequency | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| HIT Strength | 2x per week | Maintain muscle, posterior chain, deadlift patterns |
| Pilates | 3-4x per week | Pelvic floor, core stability, breath coordination |
| LISS Walking | Daily, post-meal | Glucose disposal, circulation, mood |

## 04. Toxin Reduction & Environment

The placenta is selective but not invincible. Reduce the load.

### Eliminate Endocrine Disruptors

- Phthalates (in fragrances, vinyl, plastics)
- Parabens (in cosmetics, lotions)
- Synthetic fragrances (perfumes, candles, detergents)
- Plastics with BPA and BPS (water bottles, food storage)

### Water Hierarchy

1. **Hydrogen water** - Reduced molecular hydrogen, anti-inflammatory
2. **Spring water** - Glass-bottled where possible
3. **Filtered & remineralized** - Reverse osmosis with mineral drops added back

### SMASH Fish for Marine Omega-3

Choose small, low-mercury fish:

- **S** - Salmon
- **M** - Mackerel
- **A** - Anchovies
- **S** - Sardines
- **H** - Herring

**Avoid:** tuna, swordfish, shark, king mackerel, tilefish.

## 06. Postpartum Recovery

The first 30 days set the trajectory for the next 30 years of hormonal health.

### The 30-Day Hormonal Reset

In the first 30 days after delivery, hormones recalibrate dramatically. The mother needs deep nourishment, warmth, and rest. Skipping this window contributes to thyroid dysfunction, postpartum depression, and slow recovery.

### Nutrient Repletion

- Organ meats, especially liver and heart
- Collagen and bone broth daily
- Raw dairy and saturated fats
- Continue methylated B vitamins and Omega-3

### Gentle Movement Progression

1. **Breathwork first** - Reconnect diaphragm and pelvic floor
2. **Walking** - Short, slow, daily
3. **Restorative Pilates** - After 6 weeks, never sooner

### Reject the Pressure

No bounce-back culture. The body just performed a miracle. Recovery is the work, not aesthetics. Stay warm, eat warm food, sleep when the baby sleeps, accept help, refuse visitors who drain.
