7. Component Anatomy Notes

Card structure: All plan bucket cards use BasePlanBucketCard.vue. The outer div is rounded-lg border border-gray-200 bg-white. The header is 36px tall with the entity color as background. Title is text-sm font-medium text-[#181833]. Status and type badges sit right-aligned in the header.

Color system: Entity colors are user-customizable via the appearance store. Each entity type (task, ticket, serviceCall, appointment, etc.) has a general color and location-specific overrides (remoteMeeting, internalLocation, onsiteCompany, onsiteManual). The defaults shown here are the initial/recommended colors.

Capacity bar: The CapacityProgressBar.vue renders a 4px rounded bar. Planned hours fill from left (brand blue #164387), spare capacity continues in green (#86D086). When overbooked, the spare section becomes red (#F1416C). A 4px-wide black needle with white ring shows actual hours position.

Timeline hierarchy: ProjectCardView.vue renders as horizontal bars. Company/project/phase levels have colored bottom borders and entity icons. Task level within the timeline shows type/status pills and a capacity bar on a white background.

Report aesthetic: Teal gradient frame (#0f766e to #134e4a) wrapping a white document. Header has 3px navy (#1B365D) bottom border. Section numbers in teal, titles in navy. KPI row is a 4-column grid. DAX code blocks use dark background (#0f172a) with JetBrains Mono.

Shadows: Cards use 0px 2px 15px rgba(24,24,51,0.14) for elevated state. Calendar cards use 0px 1px 3px rgba(24,24,51,0.08) for resting state. Selected cards add a 2px inset blue outline.
